About Casagrand Tudor

Welcome to Your Property Management Hub

Casagrand Tudor - Owners & Buyers is your comprehensive platform for managing all aspects of your property purchase journey.

Features

  • Communications: Keep track of all your interactions with builders and agents
  • Meetings: View all meetings in a timeline format for easy reference
  • Documents: Centralized storage for all important documents with easy access
  • Organization: Everything neatly organized for quick access

How to Use

  1. Use the hamburger menu to navigate between sections
  2. Add communications, meetings, and documents as needed
  3. View documents by clicking on their thumbnails
  4. Track all interactions in one place
